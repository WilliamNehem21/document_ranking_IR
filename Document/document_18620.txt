"Irreplaceable" entered various charts worldwide, and became the most successful single released from "B'Day". The single debuted on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart on November 4, 2006, at number eighty-seven. It climbed the charts rapidly because of its radio airplay, and it was the "Greatest Airplay Gainer" for six consecutive weeks by early December 2006. Three weeks after the release of "Irreplaceable", "B'Day" re-entered the top ten on the US "Billboard" 200.
