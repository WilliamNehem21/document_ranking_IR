The group hustled with the Mugen-Honda motors utilized by Ligier the past season, while the vehicle was really the initially expected Ligier JS45, yet was renamed the Prost JS45. Things looked encouraging toward the beginning of the period, as the group grabbed two points on its Grand Prix debut in Australia when Olivier Panis completed fifth.
